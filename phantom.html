Le Fantôme de l'Opéra.

Beneath Paris' grand and gilded stage, A shadow stirs, both love and rage. In catacombs deep where secrets dwell, Lies the Phantom, trapped in his own hell. A mask he wears, his face concealed, A scarred visage the world revealed. But oh, his voice, a siren's call, Enchanting all within the hall. Christine, a beauty, pure and bright, Drawn to his music's haunting light. A tale of love, obsession, despair, A tragic dance, a fated pair. The Opera whispers his mournful song, Where love and madness both belong. A legend lives in the Phantom's name, Eternal heartache, endless flame.
